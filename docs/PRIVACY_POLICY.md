# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2025**

OnSite Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- First and last name
- Trade/profession (optional)
- Birthday (optional)
- Gender (optional)

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- App usage analytics (calculations performed, features used)
- Device information (OS version, device type)
- Error logs for debugging

### Voice Data
When you use the voice feature:
- Audio is temporarily processed to convert speech to text
- Audio is NOT stored after processing
- Transcribed text may be logged for service improvement

## How We Use Your Information

- **Provide the service** - Process calculations, sync data across devices
- **Authentication** - Manage your account and subscription
- **Improve the app** - Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs
- **Communication** - Send service updates, respond to support requests

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using Supabase (cloud infrastructure)
- We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- Voice audio is processed by OpenAI and not retained

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Supabase** - Authentication and database
- **OpenAI** - Voice transcription and interpretation
- **Stripe** - Payment processing (for subscriptions)
- **Vercel** - Web hosting

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt out of analytics

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@onsiteclub.ca

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active
- Voice recordings are not retained after processing
- Analytics data is retained for 12 months
- Deleted accounts are purged within 30 days

## Children's Privacy

OnSite Calculator is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

## Permissions Used

### Microphone
- **Purpose**: Voice input for hands-free calculations
- **When used**: Only when you press and hold the voice button
- **Data handling**: Audio is processed in real-time and not stored

### Internet
- **Purpose**: Sync data, process voice commands, authenticate
- **When used**: Throughout app operation
- **Data handling**: All transmissions are encrypted
